Event Background

Indonesia-Australia Prosperity Expo 2025 is a visionary initiative aimed at collaborating and strengthening the long-standing partnership between Indonesia and Australia. Rooted in shared history and shared aspirations, the Expo is designed to optimize synergies in cultural diversity, economic growth, and sustainable collaboration.

The relationship between Indonesia and Australia has developed into a strategic partnership in various fields, including trade, education, and culture. The Expo builds on this foundation by integrating aspects of culture, tourism, trade, and investment to drive mutual growth.

More than just an event, Prosperity Expo 2025 is a platform to celebrate the close ties between the two countries, showcase shared ambitions, and affirm commitments to a more prosperous future.

Promoting Mutual Benefits of IA-CEPA

The Indonesia-Australia Prosperity Expo 2025 will play a strategic role in addressing misconceptions about the Indonesia-Australia Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (IA-CEPA), highlighting balanced opportunities and mutual benefits.

For Indonesia:
  • Expanding market access for automotive, textiles, food products, and fisheries
  • Promoting investment opportunities in manufacturing, energy, and technology
  • Fostering collaboration in education and skills development
For Australia:
  • Strengthening partnerships in agriculture, education, and services
  • Creating shared value for Indonesian businesses
  • Encouraging tourism growth as Indonesia remains a top destination

Bridging Trade Gaps and Promoting Mutual Growth

The expo will serve as a platform to address trade imbalances by encouraging exports of Indonesian products to Australia, while also promoting foreign direct investment (FDI) and collaborative business ventures. By fostering dialogue, creating B2B connections, and showcasing Indonesia's cultural richness, tourism, and emerging industries, this event will contribute to achieving a balanced and sustainable economic partnership.
